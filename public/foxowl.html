Website: Fox &Owl Press

This is a website that I designed and developed in 2012 for my publishing company, Fox & Owl Press. The overall look was intended to feature transparent glasslike panels containing main content in front of large closeup images of books. I drew the Fox & Owl Press logo by hand and manipulated it in Photoshop for the final desired effect. Given my basic knowledge of HTML and CSS at the time of this site’s creation, it is not currently mobile-friendly, although I have plans in the future to redesign the site entirely.
