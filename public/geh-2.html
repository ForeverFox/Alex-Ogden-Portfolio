Website: Gili Estlin Hirsch

This is a website that I created in 2013 to promote author Gili Estlin Hirsch. For this design, using HTML and CSS, I customized an existing template to create a stacked design. The site is designed for desktop viewing and is not intended to be mobile-friendly. At the time, Gili Estlin Hirsch and I were co-creating Anna, a book of illustrated poetry, for publication through Fox & Owl Press, and this site was quickly put together to meet the deadline for the launch of the Indiegogo crowdfunding campaign for the book. This website was intended to coincide with Gili Estlin Hirsch’s Facebook page and provide additional information about the author to supporters and fans.
